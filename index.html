The Motto

The 551st Signal Battalion (551 SIG BN) is a unit of the United States Army that provides Advanced Individual Training (AIT) to soldiers. The battalion's mission is to transform soldiers into disciplined, physically fit, mentally prepared, technically competent, and tactically competent "Signal Warriors". The battalion's vision is to be the best AIT Signal Battalion in the US Army
